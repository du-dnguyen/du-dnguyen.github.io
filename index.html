I'm Du Nguyen (/zoo·win/, Dư Nguyễn), a Ph.D. candidate in finance in the Trulaske College of Business at the University of Missouri. I will be on the 2024-2025 job market.

My research interests are investments, asset pricing and behavioral finance. My job market paper examines the impact of agency-issue-induced incentive misalignment in the mutual fund industry on the risk-return relation in the stock market.
